**Letter from Aleks (Handout)**
Jazz musician from New Orleans
Wears an old beige jacket and various charms
Has a reputation for dabbling in the occult and for being an old friend of Aleks
Henry was invited to perform at the casino's reopening
Henry wants to ask Aleks about the letter he sent 10 years ago. Although he's wary of upsetting Aleks

1898 - Henry acquires a copy of the Necronomicon from an aunty and learns about the occult
1907 - Henry performs at the Kali Tychi Casino. He becomes good friends with [[Aleks Apophis]], as they bond over their love of jazz
1908 - Henry opens up to Aleks about his knowledge of the mythos. Aleks dismisses it as New Orleans folklore and superstition
1911 - The Kali Tychi Casino closes, and Aleks cuts communication with the outside world
1923 - Aleks reaches out to Henry, sending him a letter asking for information on the mythos and explaining his deal with [[Father Yig]]
1924 - Aleks sends Henry his Necronomicon
1934 - Henry is invited to perform at the grand reopening of the Kali Tychi Casino
