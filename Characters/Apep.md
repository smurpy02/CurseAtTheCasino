### Human Form
When trying to hide amongst humans, Apep takes on the form of a man (a banker he ate)
He wears an emerald green suit, decorated with scales, has piercing orange eyes, and black slicked back hair. He has a well defined shape and face, despite being concerningly pale, with sickly, yellow eyes
