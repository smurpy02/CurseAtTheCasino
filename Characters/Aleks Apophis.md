Greek immigrant, 65 years old

Moved to Nevada in 1895, hoping to use his family's wealth to build an empire
Built [[The Kali Tychi Casino and Hotel]] in 1905 as his first business venture
When the casino and hotel closed in 1909, he became a hermit living off the last remnants of his inheritance

In 1911, Aleks was visited by [[Father Yig]], who offered him [[The Deal Between Yig and Aleks|a deal]]. Aleks accepted, using [[The Serpent's Stone]] to find gold and amass wealth whilst out of business.

In the following years, Aleks saw little of [[The Serpentfolk]] that made their home in the East Wing. He dared not spy on their research or worship, only occasionally seeing their human forms as they prowled the nearby wilds at night.

In 1924, Aleks acquires a copy of [[The Necronomicon]]. He learns to create [[Elder Sign |Elder Signs]] and places one on the entrance to his office so Yig and his followers can't enter. He also learns a few spells  to protect himself.

In 1934, Aleks decides to reopen the casino. The Serpentfolk encourage the idea and offer to work as staff. They even sculpt him an impressive snake statue to place in the forum.
