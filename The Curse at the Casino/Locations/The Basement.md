	A damp and muggy room stuffed with leaky pipes, storage crates, and a broken roullete table. Hundreds of black vines clutch the floor and bottom of the walls, layered thickly like barbed wire. Upon the floor, a handful of brown snakes weave between the tangling vines. In the far corner, the vines converge on an opening in the floor. 

The entrance leads to a chamber of a temple to Yig beneath the casino.

	A dark, and ancient chamber rests beneath the opening. Cracks in the stone widen and narrow like toothy maws. Pillars, stone blocks and archways fill the room, hiding its true size. The black vines from the basement spread across the roof like a spiderweb, dangling to the floor in places, or climbing down the pillars.
	One wall of the room is carved into the head of a terrifying snake, its eyes hollow and its mouth gaping wide. From the passageway within its mouth, something watches closely.

An avatar of [[Father Yig |Yig]], disguised as a large black rattle snake hides in the shadows here. If convinced, Yig may take human form again to speak with the players. Yig has no direct quarrel with the players. They are a means to an end to punish Aleks. Yig cannot enter the office. Yig agrees to end the curse if they bring him Aleks (alive).

Yig will only end the curse for 2 players. They must fight, quarrel, negotiate or gamble between themselves to decide.
