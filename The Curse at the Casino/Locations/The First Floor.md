## The West Wing
Used to accommodate guests of the hotel
## The Lounge
Found between the West and East wings. The door to the East Wing is locked tightly. The Lounge is small and rarely used
## The East Wing
Degenerate Serpentfolk occasionally slink in and out of rooms here. Various snakes slither along the hallway. None will take note of the players, as they are more accustomed to their private lives and do not interact with humans.
### Room 27
Door Locked. Painful murmurs can be heard from within.
[[Jack Feldman]] is tied up captive inside.
When he snuck into the hotel, Salazar caught him.
[[The Serpentfolk]] have been using [[Black Lotus Powder]] to interrogate him, leaving him hallucinating and indefinitely insane.
He is agitated and paranoid that players may be Serpentfolk trying to manipulate him.

A vial of [[Black Lotus Powder]] can be found in a desk drawer. There is only one dose left, as well as a note on what the substance is.
### Room 34
A foul odour comes from this room. This is where the serpentfolk leave the remains of bodies they consume for their disguises.
