The "Well of Fortune"
Owned by [[Aleks Apophis]]

Constructed in 1905 on a secluded highway outside of Vegas using generational wealth
It quickly became a popular and lucrative establishment
When gambling was banned in Nevada in 1909, the casino shut down. The hotel failed to stay open long.

In 1931 gambling was relegalised, and then in 1933 prohibition ended
Casinos begin to pop up in Vegas like wildfire, and became wildly successful
The Kali Tychi Casino reopened in 1934
