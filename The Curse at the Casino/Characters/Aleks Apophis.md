Greek immigrant, 65 years old

Moved to Nevada in 1895, hoping to use his family's wealth to build an empire
Built [[The Kali Tychi Casino and Hotel]] in 1905 as his first business venture
When the casino and hotel closed in 1909, he became a hermit living off the last remnants of his inheritance

In 1911, Aleks was visited by [[Father Yig]], who offered him great power in return for making the casino a home for his children. Aleks agreed. Yig granted him a [[Staff of Yig]] and [[The Serpent's Stone]] - powerful artefacts used to help Aleks find great wealth.

In 1924, Aleks acquires a copy of [[The Necronomicon]]. He learns to create [[Elder Sign | Elder Signs]] and places one on the entrance to his office so Yig and his followers can't enter.

In 1934, Aleks decides to reopen the casino, despite knowing it breaks his deal with Yig. However, he has not seen Yig since they met in 1911. He uses the staff and stone to force [[The Serpentfolk]] to act as staff.
