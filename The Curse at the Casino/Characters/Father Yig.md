### Human Form
When trying to hide amongst humans, Yig takes on the form of a man named Apep
He wears an emerald green suit, decorated with scales, has piercing orange eyes, and black slicked back hair. He has a well defined shape and face, despite being concerningly pale, with sickly eyes

### The Deal
Yig made a deal with [[Aleks Apophis]], granting him the [[Staff of Yig]] and [[The Serpent's Stone]] in exchange for homing his children at [[The Kali Tychi Casino and Hotel]]. The deal included that Aleks not reopen the business.
Upon breaking the deal, Yig confronts Aleks, possibly giving him a final chance, or simply warning him of what he has done. Henceforth, Aleks and his guests suffer [[The Curse of Yig]]
