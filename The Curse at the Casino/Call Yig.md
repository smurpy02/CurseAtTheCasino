Cost: 1D10 Sanity; 1 MP/% chance of success (100 is always a fail)
Time: 1 Minute/Magic Point
Requires [[The Ophidian Eye]] to complete

The chant must be said continuously. If the ritual reaches 100 minutes and [[The Ophidian Eye]] is not present, the chanting must continue to maintain the ritual until it is, or else it must be redone.
