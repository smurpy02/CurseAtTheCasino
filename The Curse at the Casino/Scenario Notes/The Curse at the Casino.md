## BRAINSTORM
The degenerate serpentfolk want to reawaken a full serpentfolk
The Serpentfolk are luring guests to consume or test on
Big Snake Monster in hotel

The Serpentfolk perform a ritual that enacts the transformative effects of Yig's curse on 7 random humans within a close vicinity. The ritual can only be performed every 1000 years. Before the transformation ends, the victims must be sacrificed at the ritual site. If all of the victims are sacrificed, it will awaken a pure serpentfolk to usher forth a new age of serpents.
