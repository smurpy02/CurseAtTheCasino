	A dim room with curtains pulled closed and the scent of incense thick in the air. Dozens of black vines clutch the ceiling and tops of the walls, layered thickly like barbed wire. Upon the floor, dozens of black, green and brown snakes slither between the carpet and stone floor.
	In the centre of the room is tall stone statue of a man with a serpent's head, scaled body, and flowing robe. Their features are not degenerate like most of the serpentfolk in the hotel, and their serpent features are far more prominent than even Apep.
