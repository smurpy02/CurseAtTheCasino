A pale stone that can be held in the palm of one hand. A snake's face, with its mouth opened and fangs bared, is carved into one side. On the other is its twisting body and tail, wrapped tightly around a bone.
## Effects
Whilst the stone is on your person, you gain great luck.

Upon first holding the stone you gain **20 luck**. You lose this luck when the stone is not on your person, and regain it when you reacquire the stone. If spent, the luck cannot be received again.

You find yourself in possession of great fortune and wealth. You may find gold beneath your land, win the lottery, or find your true love. Whilst in possession of the stone, [[Aleks Apophis]] found gold beneath his casino, granting him enough wealth to live long term.
