Infected individuals undergo transformations similar/identical to that seen in [[The Curse of Yig]]
If inhaled: make Constitution check
Fail: Gain minor transformative effect and continue making checks
Success: No effect. Immune for 1 hour. (only if not already infected. successes after infection only slow transformation. No immunity)

If ingested: make EXTREME Constitution check
Fail (Already infected): Full transformation
Fail (Not infected): Become infected and take major transformation
Success: No effect. Immune for 1 hour
