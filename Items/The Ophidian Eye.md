The eye is a small manifestation of Yig himself. He can watch its surroundings.
The eye also shows a person's true form - for example a serpentfolk in diguise - when looked through
The eye is also a key component of [[The Ritual]]

It appears as a blood red jewel, not unlike a ruby. A black orb of smoke within seems to follow whoever watches it. When held to one's eye, the smoke dissipates.
