# The Plot
Degenerate Serpentfolk are hijacking *The Grand Reopening* of The Kali Tychi Casino. They've sabotaged a fog machine with a toxin to transform guests into Serpent Hybrids. In some cases, when the serpentfolk notice guests transforming, they lure them upstairs and use concentrated doses to rapidly accelerate the process. Fully transformed hybrids become subservient to the serpentfolk.
# The Hook
Players are guests or staff at The Kali Tychi Casino during *The Grand Reopening*. They are also unfortunate victims of the transformative toxin.
# No Escape
The car park for the casino is found a 5 minute walk from the entrance. Most guests would have caught a taxi or had a valet park their car. 1D6+1 Constrictor Snakes lurk within the car park, attacking any who try to leave. Escapees may even find them in their car.

Players who consider fleeing or calling the police come to a realisation. Their transformation is accelerating and will surely be complete by dawn. Regular medicine cannot cure this. If they leave the casino, or law enforcement becomes involved, they may lose any chance of finding a cure.
# [[The Ritual]]
The Serpentfolk are performing a ritual in the Penthouse. Hybrids have been forced to lie on a ritual circle, with The Ophidian Eye placed at the centre. When the ritual is complete, the hybrids will be turned into a malformed snake monster designed to wreak havoc on the casino.

If the players succeed in thwarting the ritual, the serpentfolk immediately become hostile. Having no reason to spare the players.
# Mitigating Bad Luck
## Players Leave Early
*Players leave the hotel before learning about the ritual*
The Transformation accelerates whilst away from the ritual site
The serpentfolk follow them, having noticed earlier in the evening that they're a potential victim for the ritual
Aleks himself chases the players down, knowing they're part of the ritual and he offers them help
## Transformation Cure
*They find a means of ending the transformation early*
Finding an alternative or medical cure should be extremely difficult. Failure in a medicine roll should have great risk
The Ritual is going ahead regardless. [[Amelia Chess]] learns about the ritual and begs the players for help, attaching herself to them
If exposed to the mists, the transformation begins again
## Ending the Ritual Early
*They find a way to stop the ritual early. i.e. get rid of the Ophidian Eye, Stop the transformations*
Apep can call an avatar of Yig to take revenge. Use the same stats as for the creature regardless
A form of the ritual can take place but weaken the creature for completing it early or not using the eye
## Player Despair
*The players are stuck or don't know what to do*
Aleks Exposition Apophis to the rescue
If late enough, The Serpentfolk complete the ritual and begin their attack
## Death
*A player is killed by a Serpentfolk or other means*
the transformative effects ressurect the player in a zombified state
Aleks Apophis may know a ressurection spell, but can only cast it once
The players are free to play other NPCs (or even Salazar if interested)
