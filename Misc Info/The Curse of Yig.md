### Transformation
Those that wrong Yig or his children often suffer the effects of this vile transformation.
[[The Serpentfolk]] have found a way to mimic its effect biologically
The curse causes an individual to transform into a horrific serpent hybrid.
When the curse first takes effect, make a constitution roll
Make subsequent constitution rolls at regular intervals. Once per hour at first. 2-3 per hour as the night draws on
#### Con Check
Failure: Gain minor transformation
Fumble: Major transformation
3 Minor Transformations: Failures now give Major Transformations
3 Major Transformations: Failed con check gives full transformation

| 1D6 | Minor Transformation                       |
| --- | ------------------------------------------ |
| 1   | Eye's become dark with yellow pigmentation |
| 2   | Scaly patch on wrists, chest or legs       |
| 3   | Hair clumps falling out                    |
| 4   | Tail nub                                   |
| 5   | Fangs                                      |
| 6   | Slightly forked tongue (lisp develops)     |

| 1D6 | Major Transformation            |
| --- | ------------------------------- |
| 1   | Full tail                       |
| 2   | Clawed hands                    |
| 3   | Fully scaled arms               |
| 4   | Serpent head*                   |
| 5   | Hunched posture and scaled legs |
| 6   | Two minor transformations       |
\* nose retracted, ears retracted, scaled head, no hair, inhuman speech
## Visions
Infected guests may suffer visions. 0/1D6 sanity loss to see vision
**Vision Examples**
A large snake is chasing you through the casino
A snake erupts from a nearby guest's head, killing them
A snake crawls out of your own mouth, before moving as though to get behind your eye
A swarm of snakes wrap around your limbs, ready to pull you apart or suffocate you, whichever comes first
Other guests or staff (possibly serpentfolk in disguise) appear as snake hybrids
