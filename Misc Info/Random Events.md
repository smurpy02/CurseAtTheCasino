# Casino Floor

>[!INFO] The Poor Barkeep
>*Leon gets taken*
>Leon Goodrich was infected by the toxin. He has an "itch" on the back of his neck. Keen eyes can spot that scales have begun to grow there. When Salazar notices as such, he offers Leon a drink because he "looks terrible". The drink contains Domination Syrum. Salazar suggests Leon can have a lie down in Salazar's quarters.

>[!INFO] Salazar's Keen Eye
>*Salazar notices one of the players' transformation*
>Determined either by a luck, stealth or constitution roll, whichever is most appropriate. Salazar notices the transformative effects on one of the players. He begins making attempts to lure them away so he can complete their transformation.
>"You look sick. You should rest"
>"Can I have a private word"
>"I have a lead for your investigation"

>[!INFO] A Full Transformation
>*A guest fully transforms into a hybrid*
>Henry Broussard or another victim suddenly succumbs to the curse's full effect. Without a serpentfolk to command them, they begin attacking guests indiscriminantly

>[!INFO] Snake Attack
>*Snakes attack the guests*
>Late in the evening, the serpentfolk release venomous snakes onto the casino floor. The snakes are imbued with a concentrated dose of the transformation toxin.
>1D3+1 Venomous snakes find their way onto the casino floor, attacking guests
>only 10% of the guests are transforming and will undergo full transformation on a failed extreme Con check. A random bitten guest has a 40% chance of being one of these (for dramatic effect). After the first, reduce chance to 10%
>Other guests begin transformation on a failed extreme Con check
>If a player is bitten, and fail an extreme Con check, they undergo one of the major transformations

# The Hotel

>[!INFO] Salazar's Inquiry
>*Salazar Interrogates the Players*
>On a fumble or poor luck check, Salazar may run into the players in confidential quarters or acting suspiciously. Salazar may assume they are disguised serpentfolk at first, but continued deception will require rolls

>[!INFO] Visions
>*Players see horrific visions*
>The transformation process also effects one's mind. Players may be prone to see visions. A massive snake, Yig himself, the empire of Valusia. Visions tend to cause 0/1D6 sanity loss
