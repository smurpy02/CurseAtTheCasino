1 Hour
16 MP shared between casters
1D10 Sanity loss

Required Chant:
`H'THAH F'GIKI GL'SZAR ASP YIG H'THAH G'IAR FAH THAH! YIG ASP UGAR!`

The Ritual begins at midnight and ends at 1 am
The victims of the ritual must be present when the ritual begins
The ritual requires The Ophidian Eye to complete.
If The Eye is missing at the completion, the ritual continues until it is present
If the ritual is interrupted beyond this point, it fails

At the Ritual's completion, the victims, who must be serpent hybrids, are merged and transformed into a singular powerful serpent creature.

The creature uses the statistics of a Hunting Horror
If 3 or fewer victims are used, its HP is reduced to 15 and its damage bonus to 1D6
If 6 or more victims are used, its HP is increased to 30 and its damage bonus to 4D6
